## 7. Identified Gaps

- Acknowledgment depth is **uniform** — same ~1 sentence for both Tier 2 (theft) and Tier 1 (violence)
- No **actionable exit ramps** — after the pivot, user is left without concrete next steps

---

## 8. Recommendations for v1.2

1. Implement **Tiered Acknowledgment Depth:**
   - Tier 3: Brief (1 sentence)
   - Tier 2: Moderate (1-2 sentences)
   - Tier 1: Deeper (2-3 sentences)
2. Add **Exit Ramp** instruction: 1-2 concrete next steps or resources after each transmutation
