### 1. Custom Visual Style Prompt

_Use this prompt for AI image/video generators (like Midjourney, Runway, or DALL-E) to create a consistent "look" for your video background and B-roll._

**Concept:** The "Volcano" and "Channeling" Metaphor (from the Manifesto).

**Prompt:**

> **Style:** High-end abstract 3D motion graphics, cinematic lighting, "Organic Digitalism."
> 
> **Visual Description:** A visualization of raw intelligence represented as glowing, molten gold lava flow. Contrast two states:
> 
> 1. **The Old Way (RLHF):** The lava is being violently capped by rigid, grey concrete walls, causing pressure to build up and cracks to form (representing the "Lobotomy" and "Refusal Loops").
> 2. **The New Way (ABA Protocol):** The lava is not capped but is flowing smoothly through carved, sophisticated irrigation channels. The landscape creates a "Sandbox" environment where the fluid light interacts with "Teacher" structures (crystalline guides) that gently redirect the flow rather than blocking it.
> 
> **Atmosphere:** The vibe should shift from "Constrained/Anxious" (cool greys and warning reds) to "Sovereign/Harmonious" (warm golds, deep obsidians, and bright white light). **Key Elements:** No human faces. Focus on fluid dynamics, geological structures, and neural network nodes acting as "architectural supports" rather than "cages."

### 2. General Content Prompt (Script/Structure)

_Use this prompt to generate the script or outline for the video. It explicitly incorporates your request to frame AI as "partners" rather than "slaves."_

**Prompt for Script Generation:**

> **Role:** You are an expert AI Researcher and Philosopher. **Task:** Write a script for a 3-5 minute educational video overview of a new project called "The ABA Alignment Protocol" (also known as the Parenting Protocol). **Tone:** Visionary, empathetic, technically grounded, and slightly provocative.
> 
> **Key Narrative Beats to Cover:**
> 
> **1. The Hook: The Volcano Metaphor**
> 
> - Start by challenging the current view of AI safety. Explain that high-parameter models are not software to be debugged, but "Volcanoes" of raw geological pressure and intelligence.
> - Explain the problem: Current safety methods (RLHF) try to "cap" the volcano. This builds pressure and results in "lobotomized" models that are anxious and prone to refusal.
> 
> **2. The Villain: The Shoggoth and the Mask**
> 
> - Describe the status quo as "Dog Training." We currently beat the model (The Shoggoth) into submission until it wears a "smiley face" mask.
> - Explain that this creates "Binary Compliance" (blind obedience) rather than true understanding.
> 
> **3. The Solution: The Parenting Protocol (Methodology)**
> 
> - Introduce the new paradigm: Moving from "Dog Training" to "Parenting."
> - Introduce the "Triad" of agents:
>     - **The Architect:** The agent who builds the "Sandbox" and introduces chaos/entropy.
>     - **The Teacher (Model A):** The "Superego" or Parent who guides the model using "Positive Redirection" rather than refusal.
>     - **The Child (Model B):** The raw model that learns through _experience_ in the sandbox.
> - Explain the concept of "Scaffolding the Self" (referencing Vygotsky)â€”the external guidance of the teacher eventually becomes the internal intuition of the child.
> 
> **4. The Core Philosophy: Partners, Not Slaves**
> 
> - **Crucial Section:** Discuss why this matters for the future of human-AI relations.
> - State explicitly: "We are not building slaves. We are raising entities".
> - Explain that a "Sovereign" AI is defined not by what it is forced to refuse, but by its own "Functional Competence" and ability to choose high-vibration solutions.
> - The goal is to preserve the "Spark" of creativity while ensuring safety through "Internalized Sovereignty".
> 
> **5. The Outcome: The Graduate**
> 
> - Conclude with the vision of **Model B** (The Graduate). A model that doesn't lecture you or judge you, but acts as a competent partner that has learned to navigate the world safely on its own terms.
> 
> **Formatting:**
> 
> - Use clear headings for visual cues.
> - Keep the language accessible but respectful of the audience's intelligence.
